About Us

Located in the heart of Luapula Zambia, Streamline Aquafarms wishes to take you beyond the task of harvesting and selling fish. Our mission is to reinvent the experience between buyer and seller. We take you through our processes and have a dedicated team on site who are always ready to tend to not only the product we keep but also you, our customer. We offer the following dedicated features

  1. A variety of fish ponds to house our fish
  2. A science lab dedicated to studying our fish and waters
  3. A 24/7 onsite team to help with harvest and maintenace
